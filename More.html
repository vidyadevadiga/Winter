Topics for competetive exams

English

English is a West Germanic language of the Indo-European language family, originally spoken by the inhabitants of early medieval England. It is the most commonly used language around the world.

Aptitude

Aptitude is inborn potential to do certain kinds of work whether developed or undeveloped. Ability is developed knowledge, understanding, learnt or acquired abilities or attitude.

Reasoning

Reasoning is associated with the act of thinking and cognition, and involves one's intellect. The field of logic studies the ways in which humans can use formal reasoning to produce logically valid arguments.

General knowledge

General knowledge is information that has been accumulated over time through various mediums. General knowledge is an essential component of crystallized intelligence.

Computer fundamentals

A Computer can be defined or described as a machine or device which can work with information such as to store, retrieve, manipulate, and process data.

Current affairs

Current affairs is a genre of broadcast journalism where the emphasis is made on the detailed analysis and discussion of the news stories that have recently occured/happend or are ongoing at the time of broadcast.

Management

Management is the act of getting people together to accomplish desired goals and objectives using available resources efficiently and effectively.

Geography

Geography is a field of science devoted to the study of the lands, features, inhabitants, and phenomena of the Earth and planets.

History

History is the study of change and development in society over time.History is a process of enquiry where we conduct or investigate about the events that has already occured
